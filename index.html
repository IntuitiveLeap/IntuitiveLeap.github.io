Workmanship

Our products have workmanship guidelines to ensure that the material produced is fit for use by our customers.

  • Your department Lead, Supervisor or Manager can point you to documentation or provide direct guidance to help you fulfill your duties to our customers and to your peers.
  • Quality workmanship can be a source of personal achievement.
  • A partial listing of our workmanship guidelines can be found by clicking the button below.

Workmanship Guidelines

Measurements

Performing accurate measurements and recording results are important tasks for all of us.

  • If you need a tool issued or replaced, check the facility's vending machines near the front door. Tape measures and some other common tools are readily available from these machines.
  • For other tools, you should contact your department's Supervisor or the MRO Office (our on-site 'Quartermaster') to check for availability.
