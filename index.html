Need a Tool?

Taking proper care of tools issued to you is important!

  • Be sure to store tools in a safe location when not in use; routinely lubricate tools that need lubrication (calipers, micrometers, etc.); always keep your tools clean and free of debris.

  • If you need a tool issued or replaced, the first place to search is in the facility vending machines near the front door. Tape measures and some other tools are readily available from these machines. For other tools, you should contact your department's Supervisor or the MRO Office (our on-site 'Quartermaster') to check for availability and other relevant information.
