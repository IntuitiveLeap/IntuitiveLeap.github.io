Need a Measurement Tool?

  • Store all tools in a safe, dry location when not in use

  • some tools require lubrication, scheduled calibration and even specialized maintenance (calipers, micrometers, manometers, computers, etc.). Please don't ignore any affixed tags that call out when these procedures are due!

  • always keep your tools clean and free of debris.

Taking proper care of the tools issued to you or your department is important!

  • If you need a tool issued or replaced, check the facility's vending machines near the front door. Tape measures and some other tools are readily available from these machines.

  • For other tools, you should contact your department's Supervisor or the MRO Office (our on-site 'Quartermaster') to check for availability and other relevant information.
